About

Hello! My name is Sander.

I'm a front end developer and freelance CG artist.

I'm studying multimedia design at Erhvervsakademi Aarhus to pursue a career in front end development.

I like to develop with the user’s needs in mind and make sure that my solution meets the expectations of the user and the customer.

I have more than 5 years of experience in 3D modeling and texturing.

During that time I’ve been working as a freelancer and taught introductory classes on those subjects.

I started my career being self taught, and then later attended Digital Arts and Entertainment at Howest University, Belgium.

In my art I draw inspiration from all sources, whether it’s history or fiction, and apply my own personal touch.

I always work to the best of my abilities and constantly strive to learn more.

Get in touch if you want me to be part of your next project.
